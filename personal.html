Passions

I am very passionate about literature. This includes reading, writing, and analyzing certain texts and their elements. I love seeing how different properties work together to create and capture certain imagery, tone, thoughts, and feelings in writing.

Additionally, I find astronomy very interesting because of how different some other planets and systems are from ours, and how seemingly infinite it all is. This puts into perspective how much is unknown, which in itself, is incredibly compelling.
