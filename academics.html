Favorite Subject

My favorite subject is ELA because I love analyzing the different components of literature and how they come together to conduct and impact the piece. I find it interesting how we study these techniques and then demonstrate them in response to different writing prompts.

Least Favorite Subject

My least favorite subject is probably web design because I find it incredibly dull and lifeless. It is difficult to pour passion into lines of code, and I don't like how numb and mindless I feel during the act of writing code. It makes me feel like a robot.

NHD

For our NHD project, Marley, Rebekah and I chose William Shakespeare as our topic for the theme "frontiers" not only because of our love for him and his works, but because of his obvious hand in the evolution of nearly all aspects of culture and life. We all spent a lot of time rehearsing both the play and rap portion, and our performance ended up being chosen to progress to the state competition. We're excited to refine and expand on it by purchasing better costumes, memorizing our lines instead of using a teleprompter, adding original music, and more. We don't expect to make it to nationals, but we will do everything in our power to make it happen. We couldn't be more grateful for this opportunity especially considering how much time, money, thought, and effort has gone and will continue to go into making it the best it can be.
